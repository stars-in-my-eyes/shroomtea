Nunu's Shroom Tea Recipe

    Back

Ingredients and Supplies

  • Psilocybin Mushrooms
  • A Scale
  • Lemon Juice
  • 1 Herbal Tea Bag
  • Honey or Sugar (if desired)
  • 2 Coffee Mugs
  • Hot Water
  • Fine Mesh Strainer or Cheese Cloth, (when using a cheese cloth be sure the fabric is not woven too tightly)
  • Small Plate to Cover Tea
  • An Empty Sink
  • A Sense of Openness to the Universe

Instructions

  1. Give thanks to the mushrooms and the universe that created them.

  2. Take one mushroom and cut off 1/4 of a piece

  3. Measure 0.5 grams of mushroom

  4. Cut mushroom into small pieces and put into the first mug

  5. Squeeze half a lemon or pour 1 teaspoon lemon juice over the mushrooms
      6. (this helps with nausea)

  6. Place small plate over the first mug and set timer for 15 minutes

  7. Boil water and pour into the mug with lemon and mushroom mixture

  8. Place small plate over mug and set timer for 10 minutes

  9. Place second mug in an empty sink

  10. Cover second mug with a fine mesh strainer or cheese cloth

  11. Pour the contents of the first mug into the second mug and strain out as much plant matter as possible

  12. Place tea bag into second mug, cover and set timer for 5 minutes

  13. Add honey or sugar if desired

  14. Enjoy your tea

  • Drink one cup of shroom tea every 3 days.
